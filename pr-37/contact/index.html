Contact MetaCurious

Let’s Connect

Thank you for your interest in MetaCurious coaching services. Whether you’re ready to begin your coaching journey or simply want to learn more, we’d love to hear from you.

Get in Touch

To inquire about coaching services, schedule a free consultation, or ask questions about our approach:

Email: info@metacurious.ca

Free Consultation

We offer a complimentary 30-minute consultation to:

  • Learn about your goals and challenges
  • Explore whether coaching is a good fit
  • Discuss our coaching approach
  • Answer any questions you may have
  • Clarify logistics, including pricing and scheduling

There’s no obligation, and the conversation is completely confidential.

What to Expect

When you reach out, you can expect:

  • Prompt Response: We typically respond within 1-2 business days
  • Confidentiality: Your inquiry and all communications are kept confidential
  • No Pressure: Our goal is to determine if coaching is right for you, not to pressure you into a commitment
  • Warmth & Respect: You’ll be met with genuine interest in supporting your journey

Who We Work With

MetaCurious coaching is designed for:

  • Emerging and established leaders in social change work
  • Nonprofit and community organization leaders
  • Social justice advocates and activists
  • Professionals committed to DEI and equity
  • Anyone seeking to lead with greater authenticity and impact

Location & Sessions

Coaching sessions are conducted:

  • Virtual: Via Zoom or phone, accessible from anywhere
  • Flexible Scheduling: Evening and weekend appointments available
  • Accessible: Committed to creating an inclusive coaching environment

Questions?

If you have questions before reaching out, you might find answers on our:

  • Services page - Explore our coaching offerings
  • About the Coach - Meet your coach

We look forward to hearing from you and exploring how MetaCurious can support your leadership journey toward greater equity and justice.

“The role of the leader is to create the conditions for people to do their best work.” — This is the foundation of our coaching philosophy.
