MetaCurious offers specialized coaching services designed to empower emerging leaders and change-makers. Our coaching is tailored to your unique needs and grounded in principles of social justice, equity, and transformative leadership.

## Three Coaching Streams

### Leadership Coaching

**Unlock Your Leadership Potential**

Leadership coaching at MetaCurious supports you in:

- Clarifying your leadership vision and values
- Developing authentic leadership presence
- Navigating organizational dynamics and politics
- Building strategic thinking and decision-making skills
- Enhancing team effectiveness and collaboration
- Managing change and uncertainty with confidence
- Sustaining yourself in demanding leadership roles

*Ideal for:* Emerging and established leaders in nonprofits, social enterprises, educational institutions, and community organizations who want to lead with greater impact and authenticity.

---

### Social Justice Coaching

**Advance Equity and Justice in Your Leadership**

Social justice coaching guides you to:

- Address and dismantle systems of inequality
- Develop and implement DEIJB (Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, Justice, and Belonging) strategies
- Navigate power, privilege, and identity in your work
- Build inclusive, equitable organizational cultures
- Make ethical decisions in complex situations
- Sustain your commitment to justice work while preventing burnout
- Create actionable strategies for transformative change

Our personalized coaching provides deep insights and practical tools for justice-focused initiatives that create lasting impact.

*Ideal for:* Leaders committed to racial, economic, and social justice who want to deepen their practice and create sustainable, systemic change.

---

### Relationship Coaching

**Build Healthy, Equitable Relationships**

Relationship coaching focuses on:

- Strengthening personal and professional relationships
- Developing healthy communication patterns
- Navigating conflict with skill and compassion
- Building emotional intelligence and self-awareness
- Creating boundaries that honor your needs
- Repairing relationships after harm
- Cultivating relationships rooted in justice and equity

Our approach integrates social justice principles with relationship development, recognizing that how we relate to one another is fundamental to creating equitable communities.

*Ideal for:* Individuals seeking to improve relationships in their personal lives or workplace, with an emphasis on equity, communication, and mutual respect.

## Coaching Process

1. **Initial Consultation**: Free 30-minute conversation to explore fit and goals
2. **Coaching Agreement**: Clarify focus, frequency, and commitment
3. **Regular Sessions**: Typically 60-90 minutes, bi-weekly or monthly
4. **Ongoing Support**: Email check-ins and resources between sessions
5. **Progress Review**: Regular assessment of growth and next steps
