About the Coach

About the Coach#

A Journey Rooted in Social Justice#

The story of MetaCurious begins with a personal journey rooted in social justice, personal development, and ethical leadership. Our coaching practice emerged from a deep commitment to supporting leaders who are working to create more equitable and just communities.

Background & Experience#

With extensive experience in leadership development, social justice advocacy, and transformative coaching, our practice draws on:

  • Social Justice Frameworks: Deep understanding of systemic inequality and strategies for dismantling oppressive systems
  • Leadership Development: Years of experience supporting emerging and established leaders
  • Coaching Expertise: Professional training in transformative coaching methodologies
  • Community Building: Facilitation of peer learning circles and group supervision sessions

Coaching Approach#

Our coaching is:

  • Person-Centered: Tailored to your unique context, challenges, and aspirations
  • Justice-Focused: Grounded in principles of equity, inclusion, and liberation
  • Action-Oriented: Moving from insight to meaningful, sustainable change
  • Holistic: Integrating personal growth with systemic transformation

Values & Commitments#

The coaching relationship at MetaCurious is built on:

  • Ethics & Integrity: Maintaining the highest standards of professional practice
  • Confidentiality: Creating safe space for vulnerability and growth
  • Cultural Humility: Ongoing learning about identity, privilege, and power
  • Accessibility: Offering sliding scale pricing to ensure coaching is available to those who need it most

Areas of Expertise#

  • Leadership development for social change
  • Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, Justice, and Belonging (DEIJB)
  • Organizational transformation
  • Conflict resolution and relationship building
  • Personal and professional sustainability
  • Ethical decision-making in complex contexts

Why Choose MetaCurious?#

If you’re an emerging leader committed to social equity, seeking to develop your capacity for transformative change, and looking for a coaching partnership that honors both your personal growth and your justice commitments, MetaCurious offers the support and guidance you need.
